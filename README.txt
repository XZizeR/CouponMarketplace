*** Coupon Marketplace ***

Summary:
Utilizing login system where you need to authorize your identity in the system,
through email and password. Then UUID gives you your session token, which enables
you buy coupons as a customer or create them as a company. The data stored and 
managed within MySQL, SQL data mapped by JPA (Java Persistence API). With the help
of Hibernate the platform able to add, update, delete, sort, and show data to the
user. Also, the web application uses the advantages of Angular and REST, and it 
also has a thread that deletes out-dated coupons.
